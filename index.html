How do I register a Payment personal account?

Simply download the app, create an account, enter your delivery location, select the type and amount of fuel you need, and place your order. Our delivery team will bring the fuel directly to you. We will work to resolve the problem as quickly as possible.

What if I need to cancel or modify my order?

You can simply log in to the app and select the cancel option from settings. To modify, you can easily do it from there as well, and it will take only a few steps.

What types of fuel can I order?

You can order a variety of fuels, including Petrol, Diesel, CNG, LNG, H2, and more.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major payment methods, including net banking, card payments, cash, and UPI.

  • About Us

    • About Us

    Fuel Flux is an innovative startup transforming how people fuel their vehicles. With a focus on convenience and efficiency, we offer on-demand refuelling services through a user-friendly mobile app. Vehicle owners can easily schedule and manage fuel deliveries, eliminating the need for long queues and inconvenient fuelling stations. Our mission is to provide a reliable, eco-friendly alternative to traditional refuelling methods, saving customers valuable time while enhancing their overall experience. Committed to sustainability, Fuel Flux promotes energy-efficient solutions while ensuring top-tier customer satisfaction, making refuelling seamless and stress-free. We also provide AI models for the automation of stations.
